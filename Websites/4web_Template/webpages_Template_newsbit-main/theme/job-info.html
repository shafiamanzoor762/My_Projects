Business Controller

We are looking for a hands-on Senior Business Analyst to join our growing team of passionate people who work to achieve our mission.

The finance function supports business areas across the world with teams focused on Media, Events, Data, Spaces& Advisory. A smaller central team manages the consolidation of results and reporting to management and stakeholders

About your role

As our new Senior Business Analyst, you will oversee the business and financial management operations. You act like a consultant and partner up with other functions outside of the finance department. You will drive growth by acting as a financial steward, providing analytical insights and key financial and operational performance metrics to key people, while supporting and reporting CEO.

Your main Duties and Responsibilities

  • Manage the month end P&L reporting process.
  • Overall responsibility for the month end management accounts process, including ensuring timely and accurate submission of month end financial data .
  • Ensure financial awareness is high throughout the division with provision of ad hoc training and support.
  • Taking actions to realize a continuous improvement of budget controlling process.
  • Developing and giving presentations to stakeholders.
